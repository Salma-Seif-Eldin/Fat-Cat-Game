# Fat Cat

**Fat Cat** is a 2-player local multiplayer game where two chubby stray cats battle it out in a landfill, fighting over the last fish in the dump.

### 🕹️ [Play Fat Cat on Itch.io](https://salma-seif-eldin.itch.io/fat-cat)

---

## 🎮 How to Play
A single fish spawns somewhere on the map. Stand on it long enough to claim it — but watch out, your opponent will do everything they can to stop you. 
* First cat to collect **5 fish** wins. 
* You have tes**. Good luck!

---

## ⌨️ Controls

### Player 1
* **Move:** `Arrow Keys`
* **Place Bomb:** `Enter`

### Player 2
* **Move:** `W` `A` `S` `D`
* **Place Bomb:** `Space`

* **Pause Menu:** `Escape`

---

## ✨ Features
* **Dangerous Bombs:** Bombs display a visible blast zone — they are lethal to everyone, including yourself!
* **Dynamic Objective:** Only one fish spawns at a time, keeping both players constantly fighting over the same area.
* **Power-Ups:**
  * 💣 *Extra Bomb:* Allows you to place multiple bombs at the same time.
  * 🔥 *Explosion Radius:* Increases your bomb blast range for bigger area control.
  * ⚡ *Speed Boost:* Move faster to beat your opponent to the objective.
* **Destructible Terrain:** Blow up garbage obstacles to open new paths across the landfill.
* **Time Limit:** A strict 5-minute timer keeps the pressure on. If nobody wins on time, it's a timeout.

---

## 💡 Tips
* The blast from your own bomb will kill you — place them carefully!
* Stand entirely still on the fish to fill the capture bar faster.
* Collect items aggressively to gain powerful advantages over your opponent.
