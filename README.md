# Fat Cat

**Fat Cat** is a 2-player local multiplayer game where two chubby stray cats battle it out in a landfill, fighting over the last fish in the dump.

### 🕹️ [Play Fat Cat on Itch.io](https://salma-seif-eldin.itch.io/fat-cat)

***

## 🎮 How to Play

A single fish spawns somewhere on the map. Stand on it until the capture bar fills to claim it.  
The first cat to collect **5 fish** wins. If the **5-minute timer** runs out, the player with more fish wins; if it’s a tie, the match goes to sudden death.

***

## ⌨️ Controls

### Player 1
- **Move:** `Arrow Keys`
- **Place Bomb:** `Enter`

### Player 2
- **Move:** `W` `A` `S` `D`
- **Place Bomb:** `Space`

### General
- **Pause Menu:** `Escape`
- **Push Bomb:** Walk into a placed bomb while moving to slide it away from you

***

## ✨ Features

- **Dangerous Bombs:** Bombs display a visible blast zone and are lethal to everyone, including yourself.
- **Bomb Pushing:** Players can move bombs by pushing them. Use this to redirect explosions, trap opponents, or save yourself.
- **Dynamic Objective:** Only one fish spawns at a time, keeping both players constantly fighting over the same area.
- **Power-Ups:**
  - 💣 **Extra Bomb:** Allows you to place multiple bombs at the same time.
  - 🔥 **Explosion Radius:** Increases your bomb blast range for bigger area control.
  - ⚡ **Speed Boost:** Move faster to beat your opponent to the objective.
- **Destructible Terrain:** Blow up garbage obstacles to open new paths across the landfill.
- **Time Limit:** A strict 5-minute timer keeps the pressure on.

***

## 💡 Tips

- The blast from your own bomb will kill you, so place them carefully.
- Stand entirely still on the fish to fill the capture bar faster.
- Push bombs into open space to steer explosions away from you, or into choke points to trap opponents.
- Collect items aggressively to gain powerful advantages over your opponent.
